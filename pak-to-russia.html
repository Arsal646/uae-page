Paperwork for Cargo from Pakistan to Russia

  • Commercial Invoice – Lists goods and their value for cargo from Pakistan to Russia.
  • Packing List – Shows item details, weight, and packaging.
  • Bill of Lading or Airway Bill – Proof of shipment for cargo from Pakistan to Russia.
  • Certificate of Origin – Confirms products are made in Pakistan.
  • Export Declaration – Required by Pakistan Customs before cargo from Pakistan to Russia leaves.
  • Health or Phytosanitary Certificate – Needed for food and plant products.

Exporters usually prepare most documents; freight forwarders handle shipping papers like the bill of lading. Check all documents carefully to avoid delays in cargo from Pakistan to Russia.
